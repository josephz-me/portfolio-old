Graphic Design - Personal 2019

As a visual designer, I am constantly pushing to expand my creative toolset through free-flowing exploration. These are a series of typography experiments that I've been developing the past few months. [Page constantly being updated.]

NF Poster Series

In his song "Why", rapper/songwriter NF talks about the influences of doubt and insecurity on his identity as an artist. I attemped to apply physical contort to the type in ways that would reflect his personal struggles with public perception and smothered aspirations.

TEDx Student Speaker

I worked with Jenni Lee on designing these prints for TEDxCMU's mini events that occur before the main conference in the spring. These posters suggest that the most brilliant discoveries don't come from highly unique people but rather from ordinary people who are passionate and want to share their thoughts.
