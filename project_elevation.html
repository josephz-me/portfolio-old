Elevation Church

UIUX - Summer Design Internship 2019

Collaborators: Corey Pruitt + Digital Team
Duration: 12 Weeks
Skills: UI/UX, Wire-framing, User Research, Visual Branding

01_Overview

This summer, I was given the opportunity to join Elevation Church's Digital Team and collaborate with a diverse team of designers, developers, and data analysts. Throughout my 3 months in North Carolina, I had the privilege to research and prototype Elevation App for Apple TV, index on the redesign and launch of two full-fledged websites, and engage in weekly leadership teachings by mentor figures within the church.


Elevation Church is a global ministry with 18 physical locations and the home church of the grammy-award winning music group Elevation Worship. Being able to be a part of Elevation this summer has not only given me experience with project management and UX design tools, but also what it means to coll aborate in a creative atmosphere with right-brained engineers who think in ways a designers usually does not.

When I came on, the team was in the process of an extremely tight deadline to fully code and develop the site.
