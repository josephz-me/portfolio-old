About

Joseph is a designer focused on weaving depth and intention into our everyday interactions. Raised in the Bay Area, he has taken his studies to Carnegie Mellon where he is pursuing a Bachelor of Design with double minors in Human Computer Interaction + Intelligent Environments. When not in studio, he can be found playing with his dog, dabbling with portrait photography, and exploring what it meants to live a more intentional life.

Previously with NewTrent and MineralTree, he will be building digital interfaces this summer at Elevation Church in Charlotte, North Carolina.​​​​​​​
