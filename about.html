About

Joseph Zhang is a visual product designer who strives towards creating new experiences and enhancing the existing ones that people interact with on a daily basis. Raised in the Bay Area, he has taken his studies to Carnegie Mellon where he is pursuing a Bachelor of Design with double minors in Human Computer Interaction + Intelligent Environments. When not in studio, he can be found embarking on his culinary journey, dabbling with portrait photography, and aiming to live more by having less.

Previously with NewTrent and MineralTree, he will be building digital interfaces this summer at Elevation Church in Charlotte, North Carolina.​​​​​​​
